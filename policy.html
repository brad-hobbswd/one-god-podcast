Website Policy

The Mercy Message Podcast exists to proclaim biblical truth, encourage spiritual growth, and provide educational ministry resources centered upon the revelation of Jesus Christ.

Information presented through this website, podcast episodes, articles, studies, and ministry resources is provided for educational, devotional, and inspirational purposes. Visitors are encouraged to study the Scriptures and seek personal understanding through prayer and faithful examination of God's Word.

Every effort is made to maintain accurate and meaningful content. No guarantee is provided that every resource will remain available, uninterrupted, or free from technical issues.

External websites linked from this ministry are provided as a convenience. The Mercy Message Podcast is not responsible for the content, privacy practices, or policies of external organizations or third party websites.

Visitors may freely share direct links to podcast episodes and website pages. Copying, modifying, redistributing, or republishing original ministry content without permission is prohibited unless otherwise stated.

Comments, emails, and ministry inquiries should remain respectful and constructive. Communications containing harassment, threats, spam, or abusive language may be removed or ignored.

This website does not intentionally collect personal information beyond information voluntarily submitted through contact forms or email communication.

Policies may be updated periodically to reflect improvements, ministry growth, legal requirements, or operational changes. Continued use of this website signifies acceptance of the current policy.

The mission of The Mercy Message Podcast remains unchanged: to proclaim the mercy, grace, truth, and saving power of Jesus Christ through biblical teaching and encouraging conversation.

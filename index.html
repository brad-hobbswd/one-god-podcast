About Joseph Bennett

Welcome to The Mercy Message Podcast with Joseph Bennett, a place where biblical truth, Apostolic teaching, and spiritual encouragement come together to reveal the greatness of Jesus Christ.

Through meaningful conversations and Scripture centered teaching, every episode is designed to strengthen believers, deepen faith, and point hearts toward the powerful revelation of the name of Jesus.

Hosted by Joseph Bennett, instructor at Bethesda Apostolic Pentecostal Bible Institute, the podcast carries a passion for helping believers move beyond inspiration into spiritual understanding rooted firmly in the Word of God.

Joseph believes faith is strengthened through teaching, discipleship, and careful study of Scripture. Truth produces confidence when believers are grounded with clarity, conviction, and purpose.

Every episode explores Apostolic doctrine, biblical foundations, and practical encouragement for everyday life. Honest questions are welcomed, Scripture is approached with humility, and conversations are centered on helping listeners grow in spiritual confidence and maturity.

The mission behind The Mercy Message Podcast is simple: to help believers become firmly rooted in biblical truth while experiencing the mercy, grace, and transforming power of Jesus Christ.

Joseph’s approach to ministry flows through authentic teaching, relational connection, and a genuine burden for people. Through mentorship, encouragement, and biblical instruction, The Mercy Message Podcast exists to equip believers to live boldly, serve faithfully, and walk confidently in the truth of God's Word.

The Mission

The Mercy Message Podcast exists to proclaim the mercy, grace, and truth of Jesus Christ through biblical teaching, thoughtful conversation, and Apostolic doctrine firmly rooted in Scripture.

Every episode is created to encourage believers, answer sincere questions, strengthen faith, and point every listener toward a deeper relationship with Jesus Christ.

Through every message and every conversation, our desire is to help believers grow in confidence, deepen their understanding of God's Word, and experience the transforming power of Jesus Christ.

Mercy changes lives, truth establishes faith, and the revelation of Jesus Christ remains the center of every discussion shared through this ministry.

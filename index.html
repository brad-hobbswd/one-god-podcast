About Joseph Bennett

Welcome to the One God Podcast with Joseph Bennett, a place where biblical truth, Apostolic teaching, and spiritual encouragement come together to reveal the greatness of Jesus Christ.

Through meaningful conversations and Scripture centered teaching, each episode is designed to strengthen believers, deepen faith, and point hearts toward the powerful revelation of the name of Jesus.

Hosted by Joseph Bennett, instructor at Bethesda Apostolic Pentecostal Bible Institute, the podcast carries a passion for helping believers move beyond inspiration into spiritual understanding rooted firmly in the Word of God.

Joseph believes faith must be formed through teaching, discipleship, and careful study of Scripture. Truth grows stronger when believers are grounded with clarity, conviction, and purpose.

Every episode explores Apostolic doctrine, biblical foundations, and practical encouragement for everyday life. Hard questions are welcomed, Scripture is approached with sincerity, and conversations are centered on helping listeners grow in spiritual confidence and maturity.

The mission behind the One God Podcast is simple: to help believers become firmly rooted in truth while experiencing the love, grace, and transforming power of Jesus Christ.

Joseph’s approach to ministry flows through authentic teaching, relational connection, and a genuine burden for people. Through mentorship, encouragement, and biblical instruction, the One God Podcast exists to equip believers to live boldly, serve faithfully, and walk confidently in the truth of God’s Word.

Privacy Policy

The Mercy Message Podcast values the privacy of every visitor and is committed to treating personal information with respect, integrity, and transparency.

This website exists to share biblical teaching, Apostolic truth, and encouraging conversations centered upon the mercy and saving power of Jesus Christ. Every effort is made to provide a trustworthy and welcoming experience for every visitor.

Personal information is collected only when voluntarily provided through email communication or ministry inquiries. Such information is used solely to respond to requests, answer questions, provide ministry related communication, or fulfill the purpose for which the information was submitted.

The Mercy Message Podcast does not sell, rent, trade, license, or distribute personal information to third parties for advertising, marketing, or commercial purposes.

No user account, membership registration, or paid subscription is required to access publicly available content available through this website.

Links to external websites are provided for convenience and additional ministry resources. Those websites operate independently and maintain their own privacy practices. The Mercy Message Podcast is not responsible for the content, security, availability, or privacy policies of external organizations.

Basic technical information, including browser type, operating system, referring pages, anonymous website statistics, and similar non identifying information, may be collected automatically through standard web hosting services to improve website performance, reliability, accessibility, and security.

Reasonable measures are taken to protect information voluntarily submitted through this website. No method of electronic transmission or digital storage, however, can be guaranteed to provide absolute security.

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect ministry growth, website improvements, or applicable legal requirements. Continued use of this website signifies acceptance of the current version.

Questions regarding this Privacy Policy or the handling of personal information may be submitted through the Contact page of this website.
